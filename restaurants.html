Home Page

  • Festiva

    • "Festiva",our all-day dining establishment is a palate's art gallery, where flavors are brushstrokes, crafting a masterpiece of taste. Festiva offers an array of exceptional local and international dishes in a buffet style, along with a convenient Grab n' Go breakfast choice and special evenings celebrating various cultures. Indulge in the exquisite Swiss gourmet wines complemented by cheeses sourced directly from our wine and cheese cellars, providing a novel gastronomic encounter. For a more local taste, explore our hopper station and enhance it with flavorful relishes. Observe our skilled chefs as they passionately craft your preferred dishes in an open kitchen setup right before your eyes, adding a distinctive and intimate touch to your dining experience. Alternatively, treat yourself to a delectable pastry from our pastry island.

  • Elegance

    • "Elegance" presents a symphony of Asian flavors, ranging from Japanese and Chinese to Malaysian, Indonesian, Singaporean, Korean, and Thai. From refined Cantonese cuisine to bold Sichuan creations, this experience offers premium sushi, hearty ramen, and more than just delectable Pad Thai or Panang curry. Delight in kimchi, savor Bulgogi, relish spicy sambal, enjoy soupy laksa, and immerse in delightful Nasi Goreng. Embrace the variety and share these flavors, whether for a festive gathering or a corporate lunch. Elevate your occasion with our private dining rooms, also perfect for business meetings. Seeking excitement? Witness the artistry of blades slicing at incredible speeds or flames dancing high in the air through the captivating teppanyaki. Elegance caters to all—families, corporate gatherings, and groups of friends—all ready to relish a memorable culinary experience.

  • Taprobane

    • Taking the spotlight, "Taprobane" is now the epicenter of social gatherings.Arrive at dusk for prime views and unfiltered selfie opportunities, and witness bartenders showcasing their flair, twirling and flipping bottles while crafting cocktails inspired by Hotel ABC. From the invigorating Lemon Gin tea to the classic Gal Pol, Taprobane's cocktail alchemy goes beyond presentation.As night falls, groove to tropical house and soothing jazz tunes, setting the vibe. Elevate your experience with fusion and cocktail shishas, adding a unique twist to traditional smoking. Our friendly bar staff will introduce you to global cuisine, elegantly presented on contemporary cutting board-style plates, perfect for sharing.Step into the Taprobane experience to enjoy stunning sights, live music, and an unforgettable atmosphere that invites you to be a part of the moment.
